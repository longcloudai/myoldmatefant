Keeping people at the centre

As AI rapidly changes how we work and live, one thing matters more than ever — meaningful human connection.

Ngā kaikōrero

The speakers

Business owners, leaders, builders, educators, and thinkers exploring what it means to stay human as technology accelerates.

Trent Montgomery

Managing Director · TDM Transport

TDM Transport, a Hamilton based specialist in oversize loads, modular building moves, and Hiab lifting across NZ. Trent took an admin heavy office to AI-assisted software that dispatches trucks, manages jobs, and invoices into Xero.

Topic Less admin, more road

Adam Holt

Co-Founder TheColab.ai

Aotearoa's official Claude Ambassador and co-founder of TheColab.ai, helping New Zealand organisations move from AI curiosity to genuine capability.

Topic AI Agents

Benny Pan

Founder, InspiraEd · AI in Education

A physics and chemistry teacher and founder of InspiraEd, Benny works at the intersection of AI and education. An educator and builder championing responsible, culturally grounded AI in New Zealand classrooms.

Topic What does AI mean for the next generation of talent?

Nick Foley

Co-Founder · Longcloud

Longcloud, a Hamilton-based AI studio building bespoke software. Having lived and worked in England and the US, Nick has a deep appreciation for NZ Inc. the brand and what we can do to leverage our collective productivity to the world.

Topic Small country, big leverage
